GEO Explained Simply

What Generative Engine Optimization actually is.

GEO is not a rebrand of SEO. It's a separate discipline that addresses how large language models discover, evaluate, and recommend businesses. Here's the key distinction:

Traditional SEO
  • Optimises for search engine crawlers
  • Goal: rank a page higher in results
  • Measured by position, clicks, CTR
  • User still has to choose your link
  • Affected by algorithm updates
  • Highly competitive on popular keywords
vs
Generative Engine Optimization
  • Optimises for large language model trust signals
  • Goal: become the cited, recommended source
  • Measured by AI mention frequency and context
  • AI recommends your business directly by name
  • Affected by data freshness and authority depth
  • Early movers have enormous compounding advantage

"Google ranks pages. AI recommends businesses. GEO is how you become the business that gets recommended."

Our Methodology

How we build AI visibility — step by step.

Every engagement follows the same four-phase framework. The depth of each phase scales with your market and category.

Phase 01

Visibility Audit

We run 200+ structured test queries across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity — every question a local customer in your market might ask that leads to a business like yours. We map what's being said, who's appearing, and exactly where you stand relative to competitors.

Deliverable: Full AI Presence Report + Competitor Citation Map
Phase 02

Authority Architecture

Based on the audit, we design a content and citation strategy specific to your category and market. This includes identifying the exact platforms, publication types, and content formats that AI tools rely on most heavily when generating answers in your space.

Deliverable: 90-Day Authority Roadmap + Platform Priority List
Phase 03

Content & Citation Sprint

We execute the strategy — creating authoritative content, securing placements on the data sources AI tools index most heavily, and building the structured information depth that makes your business the obvious reference point for your category and location.

Deliverable: Published content, citation placements, structured data
Phase 04

Monitor & Compound

We track your AI mention frequency monthly — testing the same query set and measuring movement against baseline. As AI models update and competitors react, we adjust the strategy to maintain and expand your position. The compounding effect of sustained authority is where the real long-term value lives.

Deliverable: Monthly AI Visibility Report + Ongoing Strategy Updates
