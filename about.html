Why This Exists

Search changed. Most businesses haven't noticed yet.

ChatGPT now processes over 2.5 billion prompts every day. Gemini has surpassed 750 million monthly users. Perplexity has passed 780 million monthly queries. A significant and growing share of those queries are people asking AI tools to recommend specific businesses — doctors, accountants, architects, restaurants, consultants, tradespeople. And the AI answers by naming specific businesses. Not linking to a list. Naming them.

The criteria for appearing in those answers are entirely different from Google's ranking signals. A business can hold the top position on Google for every relevant keyword and still be completely absent from AI recommendations. The overlap between top Google results and AI-cited sources has, in some analyses, dropped below 20%. These are now two distinct channels — and most businesses are only optimising for one.

AIseohub was built to address this gap directly. We don't treat GEO as a bolt-on to conventional SEO practice. We treat it as its own discipline — one that requires its own methodology, its own measurement framework, and its own sustained focus. That is exactly what we provide.

Generative Engine Optimization
  • Optimises for how large language models discover, evaluate, and cite sources
  • Goal: be named directly inside an AI-generated answer
  • Measured by citation rate, mention frequency, and AI visibility score
  • AI presents your business as the recommendation — no competition at the moment of answer
  • Authority depth, citation consistency, and source trust are primary signals
  • Entity-level optimisation — building a business's AI-readable presence
Traditional SEO
  • Optimises for search engine crawlers and ranking algorithms
  • Goal: appear higher in a list of ten blue links
  • Measured by position, organic clicks, and CTR
  • User still has to choose your link from a list of competitors
  • Backlinks and keyword density are primary signals
  • Page-level optimisation — ranking a URL
  • Visibility depends on user actively scanning and selecting from results

How We Work

Structured, Methodical & Measurable.

GEO has no rankings page. There is no dashboard that shows where your business sits in AI search results. Measuring visibility requires a deliberate, consistent, repeatable methodology — not proprietary tools or black-box promises. Here is exactly how we work.

Phase 01

AI Visibility Audit

We establish exactly where your business currently stands. We run a structured set of queries across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity — queries relevant to your category, your location, and your customers' actual search behaviour. Every response is documented. We identify who is being cited in your place, what context your business appears in (if at all), and what signals the AI tools appear to weight in your market.

Output: Baseline AI visibility report — documented, plain English, no jargon
Phase 02

Signal Gap Analysis

We analyse what separates the businesses currently being cited from those that aren't. This includes authority signals across the sources AI tools draw from — structured data, third-party mentions, citation consistency, content structure, and entity clarity. Research from Princeton, Georgia Tech, and IIT Delhi established that pages with certain structural and citation characteristics improve AI visibility by 30–40%. We apply that understanding to your specific situation.

Output: Prioritised gap analysis with specific, actionable findings
Phase 03

Targeted Optimisation Work

We work on the signals that matter for AI visibility — not SEO signals dressed up as GEO. This includes structured data implementation, authority content development, citation building across the sources AI tools trust, and entity consistency work across platforms. Every action is tied directly to the gaps identified in the audit. We don't apply generic checklists. We work on what your business specifically needs.

Output: Specific, documented changes — nothing implemented without explanation
Phase 04

Monthly Visibility Tracking

We re-run the same structured query set each month and document exactly what changed. You see the raw results — which queries your business appeared in, what context, how competitors moved, and what the next priority is. AI models update on their own schedules and results shift gradually. We won't set expectations we can't stand behind. What we will do is show you exactly what is happening every month, in plain terms.

Output: Monthly tracking report — same queries, consistent methodology, documented movement
